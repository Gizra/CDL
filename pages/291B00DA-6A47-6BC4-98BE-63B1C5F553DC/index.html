LADINO MUSIC

Dr. Shoshana Weich has been documenting Ladino music for many years. In the following film Shoshana gives an introduction (min. 00:00-07:21), followed by several recordings of Ladino songs made by her (min. 7:21-21:00). Dr. Weich deposited her collection at the National Library; the last part includes a recording of Ladino music taped at the concert "Spanish Cancionero and Romansero according to  the tradition of the Jews of Thessalonica "  held at the NLI in 20.11.2013 (min.21:00-49:02).
