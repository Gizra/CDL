LETTER TO GERSHOM SCHOLEM, PARIS, 20 MARCH 1933

In this letter Benjamin mentions to Scholem his intention of staying in Ibiza for a while, a plan which will materialize somewhat later.  He also informs him of a new manuscript he will soon be sending. Benjamin notes that "this text is especially meant to adorn your archive", probably because it refers to Hebrew as well. The typescript which bears the title Über die Sprache überhaupt und über die Sprache des Menschen, also hebräisch (About language as such, and about the language of people, as well as about Hebrew) was indeed sent and is found in the Walter Benjamin archives.
