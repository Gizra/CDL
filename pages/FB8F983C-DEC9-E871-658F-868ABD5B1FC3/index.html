THE TORAH SCROLL FROM SPAIN

This is the story of the fascinating journey of a Torah scroll. The scroll was written in Spain in the 14th or 15th century. It travelled to Rhodes with the Jewish exiles from Spain, was hidden during the Nazi occupation in a Muslim mosque, and was finally brought to the National Library of Israel by descendants of the community of Rhodes in 1999.
