THE UNIQUE DE LONZANO KABBALAH MANUSCRIPT

In 2010 a unique manuscript containing a rare collection of Kabbalah lessons. some of which were unknown until now, was discovered in an attic in Bnei-Berak, Israel. The manuscript, central to the understanding of the Lurianic Kabbalah, was written by Rabbi Menahem de Lonzano in 1610, exactly 400 years before it was discovered in the attic. De Lonzano, an important scholar and a colourful figure, recorded the lessons from two famous students of R. Isaac Luria (1534-1572), the most significant figure in the Kabbalah movement in Safed (in the Land of Israel), only a few decades after they were given.
