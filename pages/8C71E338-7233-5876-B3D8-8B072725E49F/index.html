FIRST STEPS AS A WRITER – ZWEIG AND HERZL

In his student years Zweig wrote poetry as well as short texts and stories. Some of these were published in the leading newspaper of Vienna, the Neue Freie Presse. The literary editor of this paper was none other than Theodor Herzl, the leading figure in the National Jewish movement. Herzl became a mentor of the young Zweig, for whom publication in this prestigious newspaper was conceived as a literary knighthood, especially for a beginning writer.
